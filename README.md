## ðŸ§© Problem Statement

## âš™ï¸ Methodology
The assessment followed a **GRC (Governance, Risk & Compliance)** audit framework aligned to ISO 27001 Annex A controls and the PDCA cycle:

1. **Review existing policies & evidence**
2. **Evaluate control maturity and compliance**
3. **Identify nonconformities (gaps)**
4. **Perform risk assessment (likelihood vs. impact)**
5. **Propose corrective & preventive actions**
6. **Develop compliance roadmap**

## ðŸ§° Recommendations & Action Plan

### **High-Priority Remediation Items**
1. Enforce **privileged access segregation** (Clause 9.2.3)  
2. Implement **vulnerability & patch management lifecycle** (Clause 12.6.1)  
3. Conduct **supplier risk assessments** (Clause 15.1.1)  
4. Approve **Privacy & PII Policy** (Clause 18.1.4)  
5. Standardize **offboarding procedures** (Clause 8.1.4)  
6. Launch **quarterly awareness training** (Clause 7.2.2)

### **Supporting Actions**
- Approve **mobile/teleworking** and **clean desk/screen** policies  
- Enforce **removable media encryption**  
- Integrate **quarterly access reviews** into IAM processes  
- Automate **vulnerability scanning and SIEM alerts**  
- Introduce **supplier due diligence program**
